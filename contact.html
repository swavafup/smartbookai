Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 21/10/2024


Scope of this Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement describes how MCER Lab for Education ["we" or "us"] collects, uses, protects, and shares your personal information when you use our services, including online through our website, mobile app, or during research activities. It also outlines your rights and choices regarding the collection and use of your data.


Collecting and Processing Personal Data

We collect personal data through research participation forms, online registrations, feedback surveys, educational tools, and automated means such as cookies. Personal information is collected with your consent and used for educational research, analytics, and improving user experience in learning environments. All data collected is handled in compliance with applicable data protection laws.


How We Secure Your Personal Information

We implement robust organizational, technical, and administrative measures to secure your personal information. Only authorized employees, agents, and research partners have access to your data, which is stored securely to prevent unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.


Internet Technologies

We use technologies like cookies, tags, and web beacons to collect information about how users interact with our digital platforms. This helps us personalize content, enhance user experience, and perform research analytics. Details of how we use these technologies are explained further in the section titled "How MCER Lab for Education Uses Personal Information."


How MCER Lab for Education Uses the Personal Information It Collects

We use the collected personal information for academic research, educational improvement, analyzing user engagement, and fulfilling educational objectives. The legal bases for processing your data include legitimate academic and research interests, consent, and contractual obligations where applicable.


Marketing and Educational Outreach

Subject to your consent, we may contact you with information about educational initiatives, research updates, and learning opportunities related to MCER Lab for Education through various communication channels.


External Websites

Our website or mobile app may link to external third-party websites for educational resources or research purposes. Please note that we are not responsible for their content or privacy practices.


Sharing of Personal Data

We may share personal data with affiliated educational institutions, research partners, and service providers only when necessary to fulfill educational or research purposes. All shared data is managed securely in accordance with applicable privacy standards.


Retention of Personal Information

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, or as required by applicable educational research standards and legal obligations.


Accessing, Deleting, and Correcting Your Personal Information

You have the right to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you. You may also object to certain uses of your data or request to withdraw consent for data processing at any time, where applicable.


Metadata Policy

We may collect and process metadata such as device information, browser types, IP addresses, and usage patterns when you interact with our educational tools and platforms. This metadata helps us improve the educational tools and optimize research outputs.


Contacting Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding your personal information, you can contact us through the details provided in the "Contact Us" section of our website or mobile app.


Changes

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Statement at any time. Any significant changes will be posted on our website with the updated policy.
