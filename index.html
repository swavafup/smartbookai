RESEARCH PROFILE

I am a Research Assistant based in Abu Dhabi, UAE, with more than seven years of experience applying machine learning, deep learning and statistical analysis to real-world research problems. My work focuses on computer vision, medical imaging, intelligent systems and AI-enabled perception, with experience across the full research workflow from data curation and feature engineering to model development, large-scale experimentation, performance evaluation and reproducible deployment.

At United Arab Emirates University, I design and evaluate AI-driven computer vision pipelines for safety-critical perception tasks, including object detection, semantic segmentation, scene analysis and medical image analysis. I work with CNNs, Vision Transformers and GPU-accelerated experimentation on HPC platforms including NVIDIA DGX-1, A100 and L40S systems. I also mentor undergraduate and graduate researchers, support reproducible ML environments using Docker, Conda and CUDA, and translate research outcomes into practical tools, dashboards and deployable AI solutions.
