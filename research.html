UNIVERSITY INFORMATION AND CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

Khalifa University

Khalifa University, located in Abu Dhabi, is a leading institution for science, technology, and research in the UAE. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in engineering, medicine, and other disciplines. The university is committed to innovation and excellence in education, fostering a culture of research and development that aligns with the UAE's vision for the future.

Visit the official website of Khalifa University at www.ku.ac.ae for detailed information about admissions, programs, and campus facilities.

United Arab Emirates University (UAEU)

United Arab Emirates University, located in Al Ain, is the oldest university in the UAE and a leader in higher education. It offers a wide range of undergraduate and graduate programs in fields like engineering, business, law, and health sciences. UAEU is renowned for its research initiatives and strong emphasis on student success.

Learn more about UAEU's academic offerings and campus life by visiting www.uaeu.ac.ae.

American University of Sharjah (AUS)

The American University of Sharjah, based in Sharjah, is an independent, not-for-profit institution renowned for its liberal arts education. AUS offers undergraduate and graduate programs in arts, sciences, engineering, and business administration. It is accredited by several international organizations, ensuring high-quality education that meets global standards.

For more information about AUS programs and admissions, visit www.aus.edu.

University of Sharjah

The University of Sharjah is one of the leading academic institutions in the UAE, offering comprehensive programs across various fields, including engineering, health sciences, law, and arts. Located in Sharjah, the university provides a vibrant learning environment, supported by cutting-edge facilities and a commitment to academic excellence.

To discover more about the University of Sharjah, including admissions and available programs, visit www.sharjah.ac.ae.

NYU Abu Dhabi

NYU Abu Dhabi is a world-class liberal arts and research university that offers an innovative curriculum integrating global perspectives. It provides undergraduate programs in diverse disciplines, fostering a multicultural learning environment. Located in Abu Dhabi, the university emphasizes academic rigor, creative inquiry, and global engagement.

Learn more about NYU Abu Dhabi at nyuad.nyu.edu.
