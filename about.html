Privacy Policy

Privacy and data-use statement.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled across Muhammed Swavaf’s educational, research, AI, and digital platforms.

Last Updated: 21 March 2025

Scope of this Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement describes how Muhammed Swavaf manages information when users access educational, research, web, mobile, or related digital platforms. It also explains user rights and choices regarding data usage.

Collecting and Processing Data

The platforms are designed to support learning, research, and digital interaction. We do not collect unnecessary personal information. Any data collected is limited to what is required for functionality, learning progress, feedback, research improvement, or service enhancement.

How We Secure Information

Appropriate technical and organizational measures are used to protect information. Access is restricted to authorized personnel and trusted service providers involved in maintaining or improving the platform.

Internet Technologies

Cookies, analytics tools, or similar technologies may be used to understand user interaction with the platform. This helps improve usability, performance, accessibility, and educational content delivery.

How Information Is Used

Information may be used to deliver educational content, enhance learning features, support research activities, track progress, improve system performance, and provide technical or user support.

Service Notifications and Updates

Users may receive important service updates, feature announcements, system notifications, or platform-related communication where applicable.

External Websites

The platform may include links to external educational or research resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or policies of third-party websites.

Sharing of Data

User data is not sold. Information may be shared only with trusted service providers when necessary for maintaining, securing, or improving the platform.

Retention of Information

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with applicable obligations, support research or educational purposes, and improve functionality.

Accessing, Deleting, and Correcting Information

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their information where applicable and subject to relevant legal, technical, or research requirements.

Metadata Policy

Limited technical data such as device type, browser, IP address, operating system, and usage patterns may be collected to improve reliability, security, and performance.

No Unnecessary Personal Details

The platforms follow data minimization principles and do not require unnecessary personal details from users.

Location Data

Background location tracking is not used. If any feature requires location access, users will be clearly informed and asked for permission.

Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted on the relevant website, mobile app, or digital platform.
