Scope of this Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement describes how Kalima App ["we" or "us"] collects, uses, protects, and shares information when users access our educational services through our website, mobile app, or related learning platforms. It also explains user rights and choices regarding data use.


Collecting and Processing Data

Kalima App is designed to support learning and educational engagement. We do not intentionally collect unnecessary personal details from users. Any information collected is limited to what is required for account access, learning progress, app functionality, feedback, support, and service improvement.


How We Secure Information

We use appropriate organizational, technical, and administrative measures to protect information handled by Kalima App. Access is limited to authorized personnel and trusted service providers who need it to operate or improve the educational service.


Internet Technologies

We may use cookies, analytics tools, and similar technologies to understand how users interact with the app and website. This helps us improve performance, usability, learning features, and overall educational experience.


How Kalima App Uses Information

Information may be used to provide educational content, personalize learning features, track learning progress, improve app performance, respond to support requests, and maintain platform security.


Service Notifications and Updates

We may send users important notices about app updates, learning features, security alerts, or service-related changes. Promotional messages, if any, will only be sent where permitted.


External Websites

Kalima App may include links to third-party educational resources or websites. We are not responsible for the content, security, or privacy practices of those external services.


Sharing of Data

We do not sell user data. We may share limited information with trusted service providers only when necessary to operate, maintain, secure, or improve Kalima App.


Retention of Information

We retain information only for as long as needed to provide the educational service, meet legal requirements, resolve disputes, or improve platform functionality.


Accessing, Deleting, and Correcting Information

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of information associated with their use of Kalima App, where applicable. Users may also withdraw consent where processing is based on consent.


Metadata Policy

We may collect limited technical metadata such as device type, browser type, app version, IP address, crash logs, and usage patterns. This information is used to improve app reliability, security, and educational features.


No Personal User Details

Kalima App does not require users to provide unnecessary personal details. The app focuses on educational access and learning support while following data minimization principles.


Location Data

Kalima App does not use background location tracking for educational services. If any feature ever requires location access, users will be clearly informed and asked for explicit permission before such data is collected.


Changes

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time. Any significant changes will be posted on our website or app with the updated policy date.
