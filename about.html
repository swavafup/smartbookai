Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 21/3/2025


Scope of this Privacy Statement

This Privacy Statement describes how Kalima Educational App ("we" or "us") manages information when users access our educational services through our website, mobile app, or related platforms. It also explains user rights and choices regarding data usage.


Collecting and Processing Data

Kalima Educational App is designed to support learning experiences. We do not collect unnecessary personal information. Any data collected is limited to what is required for app functionality, learning progress, feedback, and service improvement.


How We Secure Information

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect information. Access is restricted to authorized personnel and trusted service providers involved in maintaining the platform.


Internet Technologies

We may use cookies and analytics tools to understand user interaction with the platform. This helps us improve usability, performance, and educational content delivery.


How Kalima App Uses Information

Information is used to deliver educational content, enhance learning features, track progress, improve system performance, and provide support.


Service Notifications and Updates

We may provide users with important service updates, feature announcements, or system notifications related to the app.


External Websites

Our platform may include links to external educational resources. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third-party websites.


Sharing of Data

We do not sell user data. Information may be shared only with trusted service providers when necessary for maintaining or improving the app.


Retention of Information

Information is retained only for as long as necessary to provide services, comply with legal obligations, and improve functionality.


Accessing, Deleting, and Correcting Information

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their information where applicable.


Metadata Policy

We may collect limited technical data such as device type, browser, IP address, and usage patterns to improve reliability and performance.


No Personal User Details

Kalima Educational App follows data minimization principles and does not require unnecessary personal details from users.


Location Data

Kalima Educational App does not use background location tracking. If any feature requires location access, users will be clearly informed and asked for permission.


Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted within the app or website.
